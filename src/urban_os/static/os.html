Urban OS — one city, every lens

A digital twin of downtown Toronto on a peak FIFA 2026 day, running 100% on one box. Pick a lens on the left; in City/Flow, drag the one lever (staggered release + shelter) and watch every lens move together.

The four lenses

  • City — the whole picture + the one lever and its combined benefit.
  • Safety — address risk over 3 fused City of Toronto datasets, every number grounded (tap ✓ verify).
  • Flow — the crowd-egress simulation; scrub time, see the crush.
  • Economy — local trade lost to the crush, and recovered.

Reading the colours

none low medium (≥0.34) high (≥0.67)

Risk uses two independent indices — Safety & Activity (0–1) — never summed (ADR-0014). On the Flow map, node colour is crowd congestion × capacity (green ≤1× → red ≥1×).
